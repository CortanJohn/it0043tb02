FAQ

What types of design services do you offer?

My area of expertise is designing aesthetically attractive and functional designs for a range of platforms. In addition to website design, I also offer branding, social media graphics, print design (brochures, flyers, business cards), and logo creation. Upon request, I can also provide animation and illustration services.

How does the design process work?

First, we discuss your project goals and preferences. Next, I create initial design concepts based on your input. Then, we refine the designs together through multiple revisions. Finally, I deliver the completed design files in your preferred format.

What file formats do you provide?

I provide design files in various formats. Choose from high-resolution PDF, JPG, and PNG for print. For web, I offer JPG, PNG, and SVG. Need AI or EPS? Just ask!.

How long does a typical project take?

Project schedules differ based on how complicated the design is and how many changes are needed. On the other hand, I work hard to complete jobs quickly without sacrificing quality. In our first meeting, we can talk about precise deadlines.

How do I get a quote for a project?

To get a quote for your design project, please contact me with a brief project description, including the desired deliverables and any specific requirements. I'll provide a detailed quote based on your needs.
