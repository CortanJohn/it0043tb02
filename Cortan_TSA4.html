ANNOUNCEMENT

No Classes on Jun 12,2024

We are happy to let you know that on June 12, 2024, in commemoration of Labor Day, there won't be any classes. This momentous day honors the innumerable efforts and sacrifices made by laborers throughout. It acts as a reminder of the value of equitable treatment, labor rights, and the continuous pursuit of social justice in our community. While we take a moment to appreciate the toil and devotion of laborers everywhere, we also utilize this occasion to recognize the critical role that they play in forming our economies and communities.

While classes will be suspended for the day, we encourage students to take advantage of this time to reflect on the significance of Labor Day and its impact on our collective history. Whether you choose to spend the day relaxing with loved ones, engaging in community events, or pursuing personal interests, we hope you find meaning and fulfillment in this brief respite. Additionally, we extend our gratitude to all members of the workforce, past and present, whose efforts continue to shape the world we live in. Let us use this day to honor their legacy and renew our commitment to upholding the principles of dignity, fairness, and equality for all workers.

On behalf of the faculty and staff, we wish you a restful and enjoyable Labor Day. May this time of reflection and appreciation inspire you to pursue your academic goals with renewed vigor and dedication upon your return. Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to welcoming you back to campus soon.

Keep safe, Tamaraws.
